Our Mission

Postal Code Lookup was founded with a simple belief: access to accurate postal information should be free and universal.

Whether you're an e-commerce business shipping internationally, a traveler filling out customs forms, a developer building location-based applications, or simply someone trying to find the correct ZIP code for a letter – we believe you deserve access to this fundamental information without barriers.

Our platform aggregates postal code data from authoritative sources including GeoNames.org, OpenStreetMap, and official postal services worldwide. We organize this data into an intuitive, fast, and reliable search tool that works across 40+ countries and territories.

What We Offer

  • Free Postal Code Search: Look up ZIP codes and postal codes by city, address, or location name across 40+ countries
  • Interactive Maps: Visualize postal code locations on interactive maps powered by OpenStreetMap
  • Bulk Data Access: Comprehensive databases covering major cities, states, and provinces worldwide
  • Multi-Country Support: From US ZIP codes to UK postcodes, from Japanese postal codes to Brazilian CEPs
  • Mobile-Friendly: Fully responsive design that works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops
  • No Registration Required: Instant access without sign-ups or fees

Data Sources & Transparency

We believe in transparency about where our data comes from. Postal Code Lookup aggregates information from multiple open and authoritative sources:

  • GeoNames.org – Geographical database under Creative Commons license
  • OpenStreetMap – Open-source map data contributed by volunteers worldwide
  • Official Postal Services – Publicly available postal code directories where applicable

Important Disclaimer: While we strive for accuracy, postal codes can change, and our data may not always reflect the most recent updates. For critical applications (mailing important documents, legal addresses, shipping high-value items), we recommend verifying postal codes with your local postal service's official website.
